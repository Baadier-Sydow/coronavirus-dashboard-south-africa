I made the website as I wanted a way to visualise data around Coronavirus in a South Africa centric way. Most of the dashboards that I saw were focused on the global impact and didn't provide a local narrative

Initially I found a dashboard created for viewing COVID19 data for South Africa made by the Data Science for Social Impact Research Group at the University of Pretoria but felt I had my own ideas for how the data could be visualised. I also wanted the data to be highly accessible for all South Africans. The DSFSI research group kindly made their data available on Github.

Some of the ideas that I'm currently working on implementing are making the website a Progressive Web App so that I can reduce the amount of data needed to load the website.
