I've used a few different data sources for the South African and International data. Both primary sources of the data are open and accessible if you'd like to repurpose the data in new and novel ways.

This website itself is also repurposable and you can find it on Github at https://github.com/Baadier-Sydow/coronavirus-dashboard-south-africa. You can create a pull request if you find something on the website you feel should be changed.

Local Data

Local data has been sourced from the data provided by the DSFSI research group at the University of Pretoria. You can learn more about their approach and why they're doing it at their launch post.

Their data is originally from:

  • NICD - South Africa
  • Department of Health - South Africa
  • South African Government Media Statements
  • National Department of Health Data Dictionary
  • MedPages
  • Statistics South Africa

You can view all the contributors to the project here.
